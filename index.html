How to play & piece guide
Goal: bounce your laser into the enemy Pharaoh. Your laser fires automatically at the end of every one of your turns — it can hit your own pieces too, so watch the whole path. A turn is either moving one piece to an adjacent square (any of 8 directions) or rotating one piece 90°. The Sphinx can't move, only rotate — that re-aims your beam. Striped squares are reserved territory: only pieces of that color can enter them (red can never stand on blue-striped squares, and vice versa).
Pyramid — one mirror on the shiny diagonal; bounces the beam 90°. The two flat sides are vulnerable: a beam hitting those destroys it.
Scarab — a double mirror (full diagonal). Never destroyed, and it can swap places with an adjacent Pyramid or Anubis of either color.
Anubis — a blocker. The armored front face absorbs a beam with no damage; hit from any other side, it dies.
Pharaoh — your king. Vulnerable on all sides. If it's hit, you lose.
Sphinx — your laser emitter, fixed in the corner. Rotating it is a full turn.
